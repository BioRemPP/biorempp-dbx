## What the Database Explorer Does (and Does Not Do)

### The Database Explorer enables:

- **Compound-centric navigation** of curated bioremediation and toxicity evidence
- **Explorer-based browsing** of Compounds, Genes/KOs, Pathways, Toxicity, and Compound Classes
- **Regulatory contextualization** — seven classification frameworks per compound
- **Guided query analysis** covering eight scientific use cases
- **Download of filtered result sets** in structured formats

### The Database Explorer does NOT:

- Accept user-uploaded datasets for processing
- Annotate sequences or infer KO identifiers
- Provide regulatory compliance determinations
- Constitute experimental validation of biodegradation or toxicity outcomes

!!! warning "Interpretation boundary"
    All database-derived associations support exploratory analysis and hypothesis generation only. Downstream experimental validation is required before any applied or regulatory use.
